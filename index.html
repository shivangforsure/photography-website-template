About Me

Hi, I'm Aarav Sharma

A passionate photographer based in Delhi, India, with over 8 years of experience in capturing life's most precious moments. My journey began with a simple camera and an insatiable curiosity to freeze time through the lens.

I specialize in candid photography that tells authentic stories. Whether it's the joy of a wedding celebration, the confidence in a professional portrait, or the beauty of distant landscapes, I believe every photograph should evoke emotion and preserve memories that last a lifetime.

My style blends contemporary aesthetics with timeless elegance, ensuring your moments are captured in the most beautiful and natural way possible.
